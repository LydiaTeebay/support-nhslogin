How to upload a photo of your I.D.

When you are using a device with a camera, you can choose to take a new photo or upload an existing one. Devices that do not have a camera will just give you the option to upload a photo.

When you select ‘upload a photo’ and ‘choose a file’, you can then find and upload a file from your device.
