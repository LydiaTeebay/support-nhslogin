How to allow your web browser permission to access the camera and microphone

Following these steps to allow your web browser to have camera and microphone permission.

  • Open settings on your device and open the list of installed Apps
  • Select your web browser from the list, for example Google Chrome
  • In the Permissions section make sure both Camera and Microphone permissions are turned on
